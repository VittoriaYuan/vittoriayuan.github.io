Game concept

Positive Effect on Reducing Work-Related
" Emotional Exhaustion "

In a world where office desks tower like skyscrapers and paperwork looms like mountains, June opens a mysterious email that pulls you both into a surreal realm. Here, humans appear microscopic against the overwhelming landscape of corporate existence. June whispers, "Will you help me find a way out?" Each memory fragment you discover is a piece of June's forgotten self.

As your guide and AI therapist, June's unique perspective helps you reflect on your own relationship with work and life. Through natural conversation and shared experiences, she adapts to your personal journey, creating a safe space for healing. While helping June recover her memories, you'll find her therapeutic insights gradually illuminate your own path through burnout and self-discovery. Her gentle questions and observations transform the overwhelming desktop landscape into a metaphorical journey of growth and renewed purpose.
